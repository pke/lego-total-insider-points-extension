## Overview

Insider Total Points Display ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with privacy as a core principle and collects no personal data.

## Information We DO NOT Collect

The Extension does **NOT**:

- Collect any personally identifiable information
- Track your browsing history
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Store cookies
- Share data with third parties
- Sell or monetize user data

## Information Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

### 1. Display Symbol Preference

- **What:** Your chosen symbol (e.g., ‚àë, üèÜ, ‚≠ê) for displaying total points
- **Where:** Browser's local storage (sync storage)
- **Purpose:** To remember your customization preferences
- **Access:** Only you and the Extension have access to this data

### 2. Display Preferences (Future Features)

- **What:** Settings such as showing/hiding money spent calculations
- **Where:** Browser's local storage (sync storage)
- **Purpose:** To maintain your display preferences across sessions
- **Access:** Only accessible by you through your browser

## Data retrieved from lego.com

The Extension reads the following data from LEGO's website when you are logged in:

- Your Insider points balance (redeemable and total)

**Important:**

- This data is **only read** from LEGO's existing API responses
- It is **never stored** by the Extension
- It is **never transmitted** to any external server
- It is only displayed in your browser while you use the LEGO website

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requires the following permissions:

### Storage Permission

- **Purpose:** To save your display symbol preference locally
- **Scope:** Limited to storing your customization settings
- **Data:** Only your chosen display symbol and preference settings

### Host Permission (LEGO.com)

- **Purpose:** To read VIP/Insider points data from LEGO's website
- **Scope:** Only works on lego.com domains
- **Data Access:** Only reads publicly available data already shown to you on the website

## Browser Sync (Optional)

If you use browser sync features (Chrome Sync, Firefox Sync):

- Your display symbol preference may sync across your devices
- This is handled entirely by your browser, not by the Extension
- You can disable browser sync in your browser settings

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does **NOT** use any third-party services, including:

- Analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
- Crash reporting
- Advertising networks
- Social media integration
- External APIs (except reading from LEGO's existing API)

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal data, COPPA compliance is inherent in our design.

## Data Security

Since the Extension:

- Stores only non-sensitive preference data locally
- Transmits no data to external servers
- Collects no personal information

Your data security is maintained by your browser's built-in security features.
